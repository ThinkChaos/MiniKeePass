Autofill

If you bought the In-App-Purchase you can now use Autofill to quickly get your passwords anywhere.
To do that follow these steps:

  1. Go to the settings app and into "Passwords & Accounts" and make sure that KeePass Touch is checked for AutoFill
  2. Open the main KeePass Touch app and open the database you wanna use autofill data from.
  3. Go to any app or browser and open a website with a login form or any login form in the app.
  4. Your keyboard should make suggestions to fill the login form. If they doesn't, select the key-symbol and choose "KeePass Touch..." and pick your entry yourself.
  5. If you want Autofill to work better, please make sure that your database entries contain a URL of the app / website domain.
